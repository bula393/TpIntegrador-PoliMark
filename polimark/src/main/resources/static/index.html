Your drive-in questions, answered

Curious about our outdoor cinema experience? Find all the details you need to make your movie night smooth, fun, and unforgettable—right from your car.

How does it all work?

Pick your movie, book your spot online, and roll up to your chosen location. Audio streams directly to your car so you can relax and enjoy every scene in your own cozy space.

Can I bring my own snacks?

Absolutely! Bring your favorites, or treat yourself to our special snack bundles and fresh bites—available to pre-order or grab at the venue.

What if the weather changes?

If weather cancels a show, you'll get a refund or can reschedule—no stress.

Are pets welcome at the drive-in?

Leashed pets are welcome at most locations! Just make sure they remain comfy and safe.
